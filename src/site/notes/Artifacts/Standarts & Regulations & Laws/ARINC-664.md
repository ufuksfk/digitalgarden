Avionics ethernet

**AFDX = Avionics Full-Duplex Switched Ethernet**  
**Used in:** [[Products/airbus a-380\|airbus a-380]], [[Products/Airbus A-350\|Airbus A-350]], [[Products/Boeing 787\|Boeing 787]] (variant)

- Brings **Ethernet** (100 Mbps – 1 Gbps) into avionics.
- Deterministic Ethernet → unlike commercial networks.
Developed by [[Organizations/Airbus\|Airbus]]
