IEC 60384-1:2021 is a generic specification and is applicable to fixed [[Wiki/Electrical/Capacitor\|Capacitor]]s for use in electronic equipment. It establishes standard terms, inspection procedures and methods of test for use in sectional and detail specifications of electronic components for quality assessment or any other purpose.
