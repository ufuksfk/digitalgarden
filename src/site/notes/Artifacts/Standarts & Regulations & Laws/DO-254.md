DO-254 is ==a safety standard for the design and assurance of airborne electronic hardware==, such as FPGAs, ASICs, and other integrated circuits used in aircraft. It provides a rigorous, requirements-driven process for the development, verification, and validation of this hardware to ensure it is safe, reliable, and meets regulatory requirements.
