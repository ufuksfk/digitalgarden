Successor to [[Wiki/ARINC 429\|ARINC 429]]  
Used heavily in [[Products/Boeing 777\|Boeing 777]]
### **Improvements**
- Multitransmitter capability (unlike 429).
- Up to 128 terminals on same bus.
- Higher data throughput.
- No central bus controller (decentralized).
