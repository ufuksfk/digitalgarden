https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hrzdYFLqMOU

1. Marina Abramović's **Rhythm Zero (1974)** demonstrated **de-individuation** through **72 objects** ranging from **comforting** (rose, perfume) to **dangerous** (knife, gun) during **6 hours** where audience escalated from placing roses in **first 2 hours** to cutting her shirt, neck, and pressing thorns into her stomach by **hour 3**.
    
2. The performance paralleled **Stanford prison experiment** and **Milgram's obedience study**, showing how **informational social influence** normalizes violence as groups lose self-awareness, accountability, and empathy, merging into a collective capable of cruelty.
