# Highlights
## Highlights from [[Artifacts/Books/How to Avoid a Climate Disaster\|How to Avoid a Climate Disaster]]
* It's 7% of global emissions, just cement. So finding alternatives is not just an edge case, it's kind of one of the main things that we need to do.
* [[Wiki/cement\|cement]] is made by heating [[Wiki/limestone\|limestone]] — the process releases [[Wiki/Technical/Carbon dioxide\|Carbon dioxide]]:
	**Limestone + heat → calcium + CO₂**
* For every **1 ton of cement**, about **1 ton of [[Wiki/Technical/Carbon dioxide\|Carbon dioxide]] is emitted.
* [[Wiki/Countries/China\|China]] and [[Wiki/Countries/India\|India]] together produce **more cement than the rest of the world combined** — and [[Wiki/Countries/China\|China]] alone makes about **seven times more** than India.
