Here are the subtle — yet powerful — ways greenwashing creeps into the conversation:  

1️⃣ Greenhushing – Companies stay silent about climate goals to avoid accountability.  
2️⃣ Greenrinsing – Targets are updated again and again before old ones are ever met.  
3️⃣ Greenlabeling – Buzzwords like “natural,” “eco,” or “planet-safe” appear — without evidence.  
4️⃣ Greenlighting – A minor eco-initiative distracts from major unsustainable operations.  
5️⃣ Greenshifting – Blaming consumers for carbon footprints while ignoring corporate emissions.  
6️⃣ Greencrowding – Joining collective pledges that sound good but lack measurable action.
