**Role:** Civil aircraft point-to-multipoint bus  
**Speed:** 12.5 or 100 kbps  
**Topology:** **1 transmit → up to 20 receivers**  
**Introduced:** Early [[Calendar/Decades/1980s\|1980s]]

- Simple, reliable, low data rate.
- 2-wire, shielded twisted pair.
- No arbitration — transmitter always broadcasts.
- Used in:
    - [[Products/Airbus A-310\|Airbus A-310]], [[Products/Airbus A-320\|Airbus A-320]],
    - **[[Products/Boeing 757\|Boeing 757]]**, **767**
