- **What it is**  
    A three‑pin device (two resonator pins + common ground) made of piezoelectric ceramic.
- **Function**  
    Like a crystal, you still need an external inverter/amplifier, but resonators often have **built‑in** load capacitors.
- **Key specs**
    - **Frequency tolerance**: typically ±0.5 % (5 000 ppm)
    - **Stability**: a few hundred ppm/°C
    - **Aging**: several ppm/year (worse than quartz)
