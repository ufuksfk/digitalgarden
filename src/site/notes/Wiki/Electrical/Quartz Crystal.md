- **What it is**  
    A two‑pin piezoelectric element cut from quartz.
- **Function**  
    By itself it doesn’t oscillate—you must hook it up to an external inverter or amplifier plus two load capacitors to form an **oscillator circuit**.
- **Key specs**
    - **Frequency tolerance**: typically ±10–30 ppm (±0.001–0.003 %)
    - **Stability**: excellent over temperature (often < 50 ppb/°C)
    - **Aging**: < 5 ppm/year
