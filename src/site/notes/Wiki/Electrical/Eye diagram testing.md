## What does eye opening show?
- Timing margin
- Noise margin
- Jitter
- ISI (inter-symbol interference)
## Good eye diagram means:
- Large vertical opening → good noise margin
- Large horizontal opening → good timing margin

## Causes of bad eye
Common causes:
- impedance mismatch
- reflections
- jitter
- noise
- attenuation
- bad routing
