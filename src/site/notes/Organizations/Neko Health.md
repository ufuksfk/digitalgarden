Neko Health was founded in [[Calendar/Years/2018\|2018]] by Hjalmar Nilsonne and [[People/Daniel Ek\|Daniel Ek]]. Hjalmar Nilsonne, CEO, comes from a family of [doctors](https://scholar.google.co.uk/scholar?hl=en&as_sdt=0%2C5&q=nilsonne&btnG=). He holds an MSc in Industrial Engineering from KTH Royal Institute of Technology in Stockholm and spent the 10 years before Neko Health working on the transition to a renewable energy system.
