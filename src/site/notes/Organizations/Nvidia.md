# Highlights
### Nvidia's Strategic Challenges
🔄Nvidia faces a **structural innovator's dilemma** as its **AI learning and inference features compete**, making it difficult to differentiate and transition to **inference-focused chips**.
🏗️The company's **architectural decisions** around **[[Wiki/Technical/HBM (High Bandwidth Memory)\|HBM (High Bandwidth Memory)]] and high-throughput optical cables** are deemed **unsustainable** and subject to **supply chain considerations**.
