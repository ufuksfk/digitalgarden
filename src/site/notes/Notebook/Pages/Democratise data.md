All data, all of our personal data, all academic article data everything possible should be public.
I took that note first time at [[Calendar/Months/2023-11#17\|2023-11#17]]

It is a website that hosts many important surveys.  
The survey questions are public, but access to the results and data is paid.

All revenue, except for minimal operational costs, is donated to foundations.  
All financial details are fully transparent and publicly available.

As users answer surveys, they can see how much charitable donation their participation has generated in their account.  
This creates a sense of happiness and contribution.
